Introduction
Techne Summit Cairo 2023 is closer than you think, a few weeks away, we hope that you are excited as much as we are! This is the second edition of Techne Summit Cairo and we are planning it big, planning it thrilling and surprising. It will be a two days event from 11th till the 12th of March 2023. The role of volunteers in the event is one of the core pillars that we always rely on; we depend on their commitment, dedication, and passion. Organizing and giving our guests the best experience throughout the event. Before applying as a volunteer please make sure to read this document carefully to fully understand your mission and role. The application process happens through numerous stages: application, filtration, interview.
What do we offer?
1-Amazing experience in one of the largest events in the region.
2-Opportunity for networking and gaining connections.
3-Experience in how to manage a multinational event.
4-Certificate of compilation after the event.
5-Free meals throughout the two days.
6-Attending any session in the event (On your free time).
